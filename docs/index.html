Core Innovations

📊

Systemic Consensing

Move beyond binary voting with a -5 to +5 scale. Capture the intensity of preferences and find true consensus, not just majority rule.

🛡️

Proof-of-Domain

Anti-whale validator mechanism ensures network control stays with active community members, not wealthy investors.

🔐

Anonymous Voting

Dual-key system provides accountability (know WHO votes) and privacy (not HOW they vote) simultaneously.

💰

VoteToEarn

Incentivize participation through economic rewards. Quality content costs PNYX, voting earns PNYX.

🔄

Integrated DEX

Built-in decentralized exchange with AMM for seamless PNYX trading and liquidity provision.

🌐

Self-Organizing Domains

Communities organize into domains with custom governance rules, treasuries, and autonomous moderation.

How It Works

1

Create Domain

Establish a governance space for your community with custom rules and treasury.

2

Invite Members

Admin verifies and invites legitimate members to join the domain.

3

Submit Proposals

Members post suggestions using PayToPut to ensure quality content.

4

Vote & Earn

Rate proposals from -5 to +5 and earn PNYX for participation.
